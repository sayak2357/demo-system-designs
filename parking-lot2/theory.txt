PARKING LOT

--> parking Slots
--> Issue parking ticket
--> Allocate a parking slot based on Type/Size
--> Available slots information
--> Payment collection(when vehicle exits)
--> Process ticket

Non-functional requirements

--> Extensible (like addition of new payment method)
--> Extensible in supporting adding new vehicle types.
--> Our cdoe should be modular ie loose coupling amog classes

	--> Length

3) Vehicle

	--> Vehicl-Type

4) Vehicle-Type: Enum
 	Bike,HatchBack,SUV,Truck
 	--> Width
 	--> Length

5) Ticket-Status:Enum
	IN_PROGRESS,
	UNPAID,

6) ParkingTicket
	--> entryTime
	--> exitTime
	--> parkingSlot
	--> ticketId
	--> amount
	--> TicketStatus
	--> Vehicle
	--> PaymentAttempts[]

7) Payment <Abstract>
	--> PaymentDetails

8) PaymentDetails <Abstract>

9) PaymentStatus: Enum
	SUCCESSFUL,FAILED

10 PaymentAttempt
		--> payment
		--> paymentStatus
